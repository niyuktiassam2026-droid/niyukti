1. Recruitment Overview

The State Level Police Recruitment Board (SLPRB), Assam has released the official notification for the recruitment of 5,320 Constable (AB/UB) vacancies. Eligible and interested candidates can apply online through the official portal before the deadline.

  • Organization Name: SLPRB, Assam
  • Post Name: Constable (AB & UB)
  • Total Vacancies: 5,320 Posts
  • Job Location: Assam
  • Last Date to Apply: 25th March 2026

2. Eligibility Criteria

Before applying for the Assam Police Constable Recruitment 2026, candidates must ensure they fulfill the following criteria:

  • Constable (UB): HSSLC (12th Class) passed from a recognized Board.
  • Constable (AB): HSLC (10th Class) passed from a recognized Board.
  • Age Limit: 18 to 25 Years (Age relaxation applicable as per Govt rules).

3. Application Fee

There is no application fee for any category of candidates applying for this recruitment. The online form fill-up process is completely free of cost.
