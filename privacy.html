Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

At NiyuktiAssam (accessible from https://niyuktiassam.in/), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by NiyuktiAssam and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

1. Information We Collect (Strict No-Data-Collection Policy)

We pride ourselves on providing a secure and private platform for the youth of Assam. Our website operates primarily as an informational portal.

  • Resume Generator Tool: Our Premium Free Resume Generator is built entirely on client-side (front-end) technology. We do not save, store, or transmit your personal data, resumes, or details to any external server. All processing happens locally on your browser. Once you close the tab, your entered data is completely cleared.
  • Log Files: Like standard hosting platforms, NiyuktiAssam follows a standard procedure of using log files. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
  • Contact Form Information: When you reach out to us via our Contact Page, we collect your name and email address. This information is securely transmitted via our form provider (Web3Forms) and is strictly used to reply to your queries. We never sell, share, or use this email for marketing newsletters without your explicit consent.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, NiyuktiAssam uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

3. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie & AdSense

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Third-Party Privacy Policies

NiyuktiAssam's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

5. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.

NiyuktiAssam does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.
