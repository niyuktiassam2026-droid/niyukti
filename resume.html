The 5-Second Rule: Govt vs. MNCs

At NiyuktiAssam, we deeply understand that a "one-size-fits-all" resume is a myth. Here is why we created specialized templates:

  • For Assam Govt Jobs (ADRE, SLPRB): Government document verification panels look for strict, clear-cut details. They need to instantly locate your Father's Name, Category (Caste), Date of Birth, and specific technical degrees. Our 'Strict Govt Format' ensures these details are front and center, completely avoiding modern design distractions.
  • For Private Sector & MNCs: Corporate HRs and ATS bots focus heavily on your 'Professional Summary', 'Core Skills', and actionable bullet points. They prefer a modern, clean layout. For these roles, you should instantly choose our 'Modern Teal' or 'Corporate Exec' designs.
